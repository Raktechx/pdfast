Privacy, in plain language.

What PDFast does today

PDFast provides browser-based document tools. The current Merge PDF, Split PDF, Images to PDF, PDF to images, and Rotate pages tools process selected files locally in your web browser.

Your documents

We do not upload, store, read, or sell the documents you select in these local tools. Your files remain on your device while your browser processes them.

Information we collect

The current version has no accounts, analytics, advertising, contact forms, or document-upload server. We do not intentionally collect personal information through the site.

Third-party code

The site loads public browser libraries that make the PDF tools work. These libraries are delivered by public content-delivery networks. Your document files are not sent to those networks by PDFast.

Future advertising or analytics

If we add advertising, analytics, sign-in, or any server-based feature, this policy will be updated before that feature is enabled. Where required, we will request consent and provide choices before using advertising or analytics technologies.
